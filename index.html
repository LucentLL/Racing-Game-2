Road: click to drop polyline points. Right-click or Enter to finish.
➕ Lane: one-click preset for a tapered auxiliary lane. Switches to road tool with Merge / Auto / Standard pre-applied.
Parking: click vertices (≥3). Right-click or Enter to close.
Building: click vertices (≥3). Auto-driveway connects to nearest road if checked.
River: click polyline points (water). Snaps to other river endpoints.
Lake: click vertices (≥3) for water polygon. Right-click or Enter to close.
Arc: toggle for Road/River. Curve = perpendicular bulge (tiles, signed).
Snap: cyan ring = endpoint • yellow ring = nearest segment (Road tool only). Magenta ring = lane center (Merge draft only — click ON the lane you want to merge from/to).
Edit vertex: Select item, tap a vertex (turns orange), tap new spot.
Select sub-modes: Whole = pick whole road. Section = pick one segment between two vertices (Delete = split / trim). Point = pick one vertex directly (Delete = remove vertex). Permanent roads support all three.
Material / Age: Toggle Asphalt/Concrete and Auto/New/Old independently of road class. With a Section selected, toggles apply to JUST that segment (mixed materials per road); otherwise they apply to the whole road. Age=Auto picks new/old by hash for ~40/60 variety.
Smooth: Select polygon (Lake/Surface/Building), tap 🌀 Smooth to round.
Pan: middle-click drag (PC) or 1-finger drag (mobile).
Zoom: mouse wheel or pinch.
F9: toggle editor.
